What We Do

From seed to shipment, every stage is handled with precision.

Six decades of relationships with farmers, disciplined processing standards, and a commitment to traceability shape the way we bring Indonesian tobacco to the world.

Sourcing

Working with Farmers

In Indonesia, tobaccos are bought from farmers through an open market that has grown over decades of mutual trust — built on long-term relationships rather than one-off bidding.

  • Open Market Commercial, trust-based ties with farmers, without auctions, keeping quality and pricing consistent.
  • Education & Traceability Staying closely involved at the source so every batch can be traced back to its farm with confidence.
  • Good Agricultural Practices Guiding farmers in Jember, Lombok, and Bali on CPA use, soil health, and post-harvest handling.
Production

Processing

Upon receiving tobacco from the buying stations, it is our priority to deliver quality products, checked against our internal standards at every stage.

  • Receiving Every incoming batch is inspected on arrival before moving further into the process.
  • Sorting by Grade Non-tobacco related materials (NTRMs) are maximally removed by experienced grading staff.
  • Final Product Forms Handstripped, Loose Leaf, Open Laid, Butted Loose Leaf, Bundled, or Fermented — to exact specification.
